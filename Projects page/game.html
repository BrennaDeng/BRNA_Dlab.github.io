“Dimension Echoes” is a platformer featuring a fragmented narrative, in which players progress through levels to successfully “escape” the tower.

Created using hand-drawn 2D art, the game draws inspiration from reflections on a silent past and the reconstruction of space. Unfortunately, due to time constraints and my technical limitations at the time, I was unable to fully complete it within the limited timeframe, so I had to cut the final level, resulting in a semi-open ending.

Some mechanics draw inspiration from Hollow Knight (since I really enjoy playing it), and one scene includes a small image as a tribute to the game.

A demo version of the game is currently available on itch.io—we welcome all visitors to give it a try!
