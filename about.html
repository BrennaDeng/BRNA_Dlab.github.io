Hi! I am Yihan Deng! Or you can call me Brenna!

I am a visual storyteller who operates at the junction of motion and illustration. My core expertise lies in 2D animation and graphic design, backed by over 3-4 years of experience in professional video editing using the Adobe Creative Suite.

Currently, my creative laboratory is expanding: I am bridging the gap between static art and dynamic environments by exploring 3D assets and Unity-based interactive systems. I strive to create experiences that are not just seen, but felt—transforming "randomness" into structured visual narratives.
